About us

This website allows you to exercise your right to vote by sitting at home or from a remote place. During this pandemic the site helps you to cast vote by maintaining social distancing and staying at home following the main motto of protection against corona 'STAY HOME STAY SAFE'. The website is user-friendly and has all the authentication features making it reliable from both the user and admin end.If you have any queries feel free to contact us using the form in the home page and we will get back to you in two days.


Instructions for user:

  • Click on Signup from the Signup/Login dropdown list and fill the form
  • Make sure you use a unique username and mail id as each of them can be used by only one user.Same username and mail id can't be used by two users
  • After filling the form click on register. If all the requirements are met you will get a success alert and will be directed to the login page.Remember you need to be 18 years to register and cast your vote.
  • Enter the username and password, the ones used while registering and click on login.
  • On successfull login you will directed to the home page.CLick on 'Candidates' on the navbar to check the candidates participating in the election
  • After deciding whom to vote, click on the vote button below your go-to candidate details and done.You have successfully casted your vote.
  • You even get the options to update the profile and change the password if necessary.
  • Once everything is done click the dropdown list next to your name in the navbar and click on the logout option to logout.


    • Instructions for admin:

  • Click on Signup from the Admin dropdown list and fill the form
  • Secret key is the key provided by the company and make sure that a unique mail id is used as same mail id cannot be used by two admins
  • On clicking register if all requirements are met you will be directed to the login page. Enter the details and click login
  • On successfull login you will be directed to the home page where you will find options to add and update candidates under the manage dropdown list, edit your profile,change password of your account and more importantly view the results of the election under the droplist list near your name.
  • In update candidate option the admin can edit the details of the candidate and delete a candidate if they decide to withdraw from the election
  • Once everything is done click the dropdown list next to your name in the navbar and click on the logout option to logout.


    • Remember that your vote is precious and you vote the candidate who will be responsible and will work for his people.
